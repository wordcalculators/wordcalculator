How this age calculator works

The age calculator finds the exact time difference between two calendar dates. It shows the result in years, months, and days, and also converts the same span into weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

Notes

The calculator assumes the starting moment is at midnight of the selected date. Time zones are not taken into account and the result is based purely on calendar dates.
